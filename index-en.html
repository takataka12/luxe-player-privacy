Privacy Policy

Luxe Player
Last Updated: January 2025
Important Notice: Luxe Player does not collect any personal information. All data is processed exclusively on your device.

1. Data Collection

Luxe Player does NOT collect any of the following personal information:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Analytics data

2. Music Library Access

Luxe Player accesses your iPhone/iPad music library for the following purposes only:

  • Displaying lists of artists, albums, and songs
  • Playing music
  • Displaying album artwork

Important: Music library information is processed exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Storage

Luxe Player stores only the following data on your device:

  • Album artwork cache (for memory optimization)
  • Playback settings (repeat and shuffle status)

This data is the minimum required for app functionality and is never shared externally.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

Luxe Player does NOT share your information with any third parties.

5. Internet Connection

Luxe Player does not require an internet connection. It operates completely offline.

6. Advertising and Tracking

Luxe Player contains no advertisements and does not use any tracking technologies.

7. Children's Privacy

Since Luxe Player does not collect personal information, it is safe for users of all ages.

8. Your Rights

Because Luxe Player does not collect personal information, there is no need for deletion or modification requests. Uninstalling the app will automatically remove all local data.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as necessary. If significant changes occur, we will notify you within the app or on this page.
